Vitamin Why It's Important Good Sources
A Supports healthy vision, skin, bones, teeth, and reproductive functions. 🥕 Carrots, 🍑 Peaches, 🥬 Spinach
B1
(Thiamine)		 Helps in converting food into energy and supports nerve function. 🍚 Rice, 🍞 Whole Wheat Bread, 🍠 Sweet Potatoes
B2
(Riboflavin)		 Aids in energy production and promotes healthy skin, hair, blood, and brain. 🍄 Mushrooms, 🥦 Broccoli, 🥜 Almonds
B3
(Niacin)		 Essential for energy production and maintaining a healthy nervous system. 🥑 Avocados, 🥬 Leafy Greens, 🍅 Tomatoes
B5
(Pantothenic Acid)		 Assists in hormone production and helps obtain energy from food. 🍠 Sweet Potatoes, 🍄 Mushrooms, 🥜 Peanuts
B6
(Pyridoxine)		 Crucial for red blood cell formation and helps with sleep, appetite, and mood regulation. 🍌 Bananas, 🍉 Watermelon, 🥔 Potatoes
B7
(Biotin)		 Facilitates energy production and the breakdown of glucose. 🍞 Whole Wheat Bread, 🍄 Mushrooms, 🥜 Almonds
B9
(Folate)		 Important for new cell creation and DNA synthesis. 🥬 Spinach, 🍅 Tomatoes, 🥕 Carrots
B12 Helps break down fatty acids and supports red blood cell production. 🌿 Nori, 🍄 Mushrooms, 🥥 Coconut Milk
C Functions as an antioxidant, boosts the immune system, and aids in cell repair. 🍊 Oranges, 🥦 Broccoli, 🥝 Kiwis
D Supports calcium and phosphorus absorption for bone and teeth health. ☀️ Sunlight, 🍄 Mushrooms, 🐟 Fortified Plant Milks
E Acts as an antioxidant and helps stabilize cell membranes. 🥑 Avocados, 🌰 Nuts, 🥦 Broccoli
K Essential for blood clotting and maintaining calcium balance. 🥬 Kale, 🥦 Broccoli, 🥒 Cucumbers
